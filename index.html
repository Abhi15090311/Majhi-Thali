WHO ARE WE?

A group of enthusiastic IT students on a journey to promote the thali culture in the country and make premium thalis affordable. We aim to convert a normal thali to a memorable experience that the customer can savour every moment of. Always striving to be the best in what we do, we present you the idea of Majhi Thali.


WHAT IS MAJHI THALI?

Majhi Thali is a one-stop thing for all your thali cravings, with a customized touch. What Majhi Thali does is that it takes different menu items from different restaurants and prepares a single thali with those items. Most of our tie up groups are 4 or 5 star restaurants that are very difficult to afford for middle class people. Majhi Thali, gives them a chance to enjoy the cuisines of these extravagantly costly restaurants in very affordable prices, starting at as low as 30 rupees! By changing the menu everyday, we make sure that people don't get bored and get to enjoy different cuisines and specialities of those restaurants.
